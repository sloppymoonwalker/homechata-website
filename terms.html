2. Description of Service

HomeChata is a neighborhood intelligence application that aggregates publicly available and licensed third-party data to help home buyers and individuals research neighborhoods and areas they are considering. The App provides the following types of information for any searched address:

  • Crime Data: Violent and property crime statistics sourced from the FBI Crime Data Explorer (UCR Program), including 5-year trend analysis and incident breakdowns by offense type.
  • Recent Crime News: Local news summaries and incident reports related to crime activity in the searched area, aggregated via AI-powered news services.
  • Sex Offender Proximity: The number and proximity of registered sex offenders within specified radius distances of the searched address, sourced from publicly available sex offender registry data aggregated by authorized third-party providers.
  • Air Quality: Air quality index (AQI) data for the searched area, sourced from third-party environmental monitoring services.
  • Nearby Amenities: Information about nearby schools, grocery stores, hospitals, urgent care facilities, daycare centers, public transit, parks, gyms, golf courses, EV charging stations, dog parks, and other points of interest, sourced via Google Places API and related services.
  • Nearby Cities: Distance and general population data for surrounding cities and towns.

All data and content provided by HomeChata is for general informational purposes only. HomeChata provides access to the App and its content solely for your personal, non-commercial use. You agree to independently verify all information before making any real estate, financial, legal, or other significant decisions.

4.1 Crime Data

Crime statistics are sourced from the FBI Crime Data Explorer (CDE), which compiles data from law enforcement agencies through the Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) Program. Crime data typically reflects the most recently available complete calendar year and may lag current conditions. Not all law enforcement agencies participate in or report to the UCR Program; therefore, data may be incomplete for certain jurisdictions. Crime statistics reflect reported offenses only and do not capture unreported crime. HomeChata makes no warranty as to the accuracy, completeness, or currency of crime data. You should independently verify crime information through local law enforcement agencies before making any decisions.

4.2 Sex Offender Data

Sex offender proximity information is sourced from licensed third-party data providers who aggregate publicly available information from state and territorial sex offender registries. Not all states and territories provide geographic coordinate data, which means coverage may vary by location. Offender information reflects registry data at the time of the search and may not reflect real-time changes, relocations, or updates to individual registrations. Sex offender data is provided for general informational awareness only and must not be used to harass, threaten, discriminate against, or take any adverse action against any individual. HomeChata is not a background check service.

4.3 Air Quality Data

Air quality data is sourced from third-party environmental monitoring providers and reflects conditions at or near the searched address based on the nearest available monitoring station. Data may not reflect hyperlocal conditions and is provided without any warranty of accuracy or fitness for purpose.

4.4 Amenity & Points of Interest Data

Nearby amenity information is sourced from Google Places API and other third-party services. Business listings, operating hours, distances, and other details are provided as-is and may not reflect current real-world conditions. HomeChata is not responsible for the accuracy or currency of third-party business listings.
