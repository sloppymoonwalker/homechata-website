HomeChata is a neighborhood exploration app that displays publicly available data for any address in the United States. The App retrieves and presents data from third-party sources and public databases.

Information Displayed

HomeChata displays information about nearby amenities across multiple categories, including:

  • Schools, hospitals, and healthcare facilities

5. Data Accuracy and Limitations

General Disclaimer

HomeChata relies on third-party data sources and publicly available information. We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, timeliness, or reliability of any data displayed in the App.

Amenity Data

Information about nearby amenities (schools, hospitals, parks, grocery stores, restaurants, etc.) is obtained from third-party sources and may not include all locations. Distances are approximate. Business hours, contact information, ratings, and operational status may change without notice and may not be current.

Air Quality Data

Air quality data reflects conditions at the time of the search and may change. Air quality information is provided by third-party sources and may not reflect the most current conditions.

Crime Data

Crime statistics displayed in HomeChata are obtained from the FBI Crime Data Explorer (UCR Program) and publicly available third-party sources. Crime data typically reflects the most recently available complete calendar year and may lag current conditions.

Crime statistics may include information about total crimes, violent crimes, property crimes, crime rates, and historical trends. Crime data may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. Reporting methods, crime classifications, definitions, and time periods vary significantly between sources and jurisdictions. Not all law enforcement agencies participate in or report to the UCR Program.

HomeChata does not verify, validate, or guarantee the accuracy of crime statistics. We do not evaluate, rank, score, or judge neighborhood safety. We do not provide safety recommendations, safety ratings, or safety advice. All crime data is presented for informational purposes only and should not be relied upon as a sole source of information for making decisions about safety, housing, or relocation.

Sex Offender Data

Sex offender proximity information is sourced from licensed third-party providers who aggregate publicly available state and territorial sex offender registry data. Not all states provide geographic coordinate data, which means coverage may vary by location. Offender information reflects registry data at the time of the search and may not reflect real-time changes.

Sex offender data is provided for general informational awareness only. This data must not be used to harass, threaten, discriminate against, or take any adverse action against any individual. HomeChata is not a background check service.

Important Notice

HomeChata is provided for informational purposes only. You should not rely solely on HomeChata data when making housing, real estate, relocation, or other important decisions. We strongly recommend verifying important information through official sources, including:

  • Local police departments and law enforcement agencies
  • Government crime databases and statistics (e.g., FBI Crime Data Explorer)
  • Municipal and county government websites
  • School districts and educational authorities
  • Local health departments and air quality monitoring agencies
  • Direct contact with businesses, institutions, and service providers
