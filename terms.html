4. Description of Service

HomeChata is a comprehensive neighborhood intelligence platform that empowers home buyers, renters, and individuals to make informed decisions about where they live. By synthesizing data across public safety, environmental quality, and community amenities into a single, on-demand report for any address in the United States, HomeChata delivers high-value neighborhood intelligence — aggregated, processed, and presented on a single platform — giving users instant access to insights that would otherwise demand extensive research across fragmented, hard-to-navigate public sources. Behind every report is a sophisticated data infrastructure purpose-built to serve people making one of the most important decisions of their lives.

HomeChata is an informational intelligence tool and is not a real estate brokerage, investment advisor, legal advisor, consumer reporting agency, or background check service. Nothing within the App constitutes real estate, financial, legal, or safety advice.

What HomeChata Delivers

A HomeChata neighborhood report provides a comprehensive intelligence snapshot across multiple dimensions, including:

  • Crime and public safety data, including historical trends and incident breakdowns
  • Registered sex offender proximity within defined radius ranges
  • Recent local crime news and public safety incidents
  • Real-time environmental quality indicators, including air quality
  • Proximity and access to essential amenities — schools, hospitals, urgent care, daycare, and grocery stores
  • Lifestyle amenities — parks, gyms, restaurants, transit, and more
  • Nearby cities and regional community context

Nature of Service

HomeChata presents verified, factual data compiled from authoritative sources. The App does not generate opinions, assign scores, issue ratings, or make recommendations regarding any neighborhood, address, or community. Users are solely responsible for drawing their own conclusions from the data presented and for any decisions made in connection therewith.

5. Data Accuracy and Limitations

General Disclaimer

HomeChata aggregates, processes, and presents neighborhood intelligence data from a broad spectrum of government, institutional, and licensed sources. While we are committed to delivering high-quality, meaningful data insights, the nature of large-scale public data means that no dataset is without limitation. HomeChata does not warrant the absolute accuracy, completeness, or real-time currency of any data presented within the App. Data is refreshed periodically and may not reflect the most recent conditions at any given address. In circumstances where comprehensive data is unavailable for a specific location, contextual estimates or regional data may be presented to ensure a complete and useful experience.

Crime and Public Safety Data

HomeChata presents crime and public safety data sourced from government law enforcement databases and institutional reporting programs. This data is subject to reporting cycles and may reflect conditions from prior periods rather than real-time activity. Coverage and reporting standards vary across jurisdictions, and not all law enforcement agencies participate in national reporting programs, which may result in data gaps in certain areas. Crime statistics reflect reported offenses only and do not capture unreported incidents. HomeChata presents this data as a factual reference tool only. We do not evaluate, score, or render judgments on neighborhood safety. This information should not be relied upon as the sole basis for any decision related to safety, housing, or relocation.

Sex Offender Data

HomeChata delivers registered sex offender proximity intelligence as part of its comprehensive public safety awareness offering, drawing directly from official government sex offender registry programs administered at the state, territorial, and federal levels. Registry data is maintained and updated by individual jurisdictions in accordance with their respective statutory requirements and reporting cycles. As registry standards, geographic coverage, and update frequencies vary across jurisdictions, HomeChata presents this information as a current-as-available reference, which may not reflect the absolute real-time status of any individual registration at the moment of a given search.

This information is provided as a public awareness resource within a broader neighborhood intelligence context. It must not be used to harass, threaten, discriminate against, or take any adverse action against any individual. HomeChata is not a consumer reporting agency as defined under the Fair Credit Reporting Act and does not provide background screening, tenant screening, or employment screening services.

Environmental and Amenity Data

HomeChata presents environmental and quality-of-life data, including air quality indicators and nearby points of interest, sourced from institutional monitoring programs and location intelligence providers. Environmental readings reflect area-level conditions and may not capture hyperlocal variations. Amenity information, including business listings, hours of operation, and distances, is approximate and subject to change. HomeChata is not responsible for the accuracy or currency of third-party listings.

Data Estimates

In circumstances where granular data is unavailable for a specific searched location, HomeChata may present contextual estimates or regional approximations to provide a complete and informative experience. Such estimates are clearly informational in nature and may not precisely reflect conditions at the specific address searched.

Independent Verification

HomeChata is designed to be a powerful starting point for neighborhood research — not the final word. We strongly encourage users to supplement HomeChata insights with independent research through official government sources, local law enforcement agencies, school districts, municipal websites, and direct engagement with relevant institutions before making any significant housing, real estate, or relocation decisions.
